Welcome to Sri Lanka

Welcome to Happy Vacation, your ultimate gateway to exploring the tropical paradise of Sri Lanka! Whether you're seeking tranquil beaches, lush jungles, ancient temples, or vibrant cities, our travel website offers a comprehensive guide to the best destinations across this beautiful island.

Discover Sri Lanka's Breathtaking Locations

Explore the pristine shores of Unawatuna and Mirissa, where golden sands meet crystal-clear waters, perfect for relaxing or engaging in water sports. Venture to the historic Sigiriya Rock Fortress, an ancient wonder with awe-inspiring views, or wander through the lush tea plantations of Nuwara Eliya for a cool escape. Journey to the Cultural Triangle, home to UNESCO World Heritage Sites such as the Sacred City of Kandy and Polonnaruwa, offering a deep dive into Sri Lanka’s rich history and culture.

For nature lovers, the Yala National Park and Udawalawe provide exhilarating safari experiences, while the Knuckles Mountain Range presents a hiking haven amidst stunning landscapes.
